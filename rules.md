# Rules, Prizes & Judging

## Hard requirements

1. Build an ASP solving a clear, real-world use case (crypto OR non-crypto).
2. Submit the ASP for listing on OKX.AI. **It must pass OKX internal review and
   go live** — if not approved/live, the hackathon submission is INVALID.
3. Post on X with **#OKXAI**: introduce the ASP, explain the use case, include a
   demo/walkthrough **≤ 90 seconds**. No separate demo video upload needed.
4. Submit the Google form before **Jul 17, 2026, 23:59 UTC** with ASP details and
   the X post link.

## Effective deadlines (work backwards)

- ASP review takes ~24h → **submit the listing by end of Jul 15** to leave room
  for one rejection + resubmit cycle.
- Publish X post + form by **~20:00 UTC Jul 17**, not 23:59.

All judging: "OKXAI Internal Review" — no public rubric. Category prizes go to
the *top-performing* ASP in each marketplace category, implying real usage
counts, not just the demo. Prizes paid in USDT; may change if few submissions
meet quality standards. HackQuest is not involved in judging.

What OKX says they want: "AI services that solve real problems, create real
value, and serve real users. We're not looking for another chatbot."
