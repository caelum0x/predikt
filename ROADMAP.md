# Predikt — How we surpass Polymarket & Kalshi

## The gap we exploit

| | Kalshi | Polymarket | **Predikt** |
|---|---|---|---|
| Money | USD (fiat), KYC | USDC (crypto) | **Both — play money + on-chain USDC, per market** |
| Geography | **US only** | **US banned** | **Global** (play money everywhere; on-chain where crypto is legal) |
| Onboarding | Heavy KYC | Wallet + crypto | **Instant guest play → social login → embedded wallet** |
| Market creation | CFTC-gatekept | Mostly curated | **Permissionless — anyone creates** |
| Resolution | Centralized (Kalshi) | UMA (trustless) | **UMA trustless on-chain** |
| Liquidity model | CLOB | CLOB (thin long-tail) | **Hybrid CPMM + CLOB (always liquid + tight)** |
| Creators earn | No | Minimal | **Creators earn fees → supply flywheel** |

**The wedge:** neither competitor can serve the other's users. Kalshi can't touch crypto or non-US; Polymarket can't touch the US or no-crypto users. Predikt does **both markets in one product** — free play money as the global, no-KYC, viral top of funnel; on-chain USDC for real, trustless stakes. We already have both paths built.

---

## Phase 1 — Frictionless onboarding (beat both on funnel)
- **Instant play, no signup** (guest → play money). Kalshi/Poly both gate at the door.
- **Social login + embedded wallet** (reuse the Aether wallet infra) → crypto with no seed-phrase friction; **gasless via account abstraction**.
- **Mobile app** from the same codebase.
- *Why we win:* the cheapest path from "curious" to "first trade" of any prediction market.

## Phase 2 — Liquidity (beat Polymarket's only real moat)
- **Hybrid engine:** CPMM auto-liquidity for the long tail (every market instantly tradeable — Poly's CLOB can't) **+** CLOB for deep markets (tight spreads — Manifold's CPMM can't). We have both pieces.
- **Maker rebates + LP rewards**; the on-chain **market-maker bot seeds every market** on launch.
- *Why we win:* always-liquid long tail + tight top-of-book. Polymarket is empty below the top 50 markets.

## Phase 3 — Market depth & types (beat Kalshi's rigidity)
- **Permissionless creation** (Kalshi legally cannot).
- Rich types: binary, multi-outcome, **scalar/numeric**, **conditional**, **combinatorial/parlays**, live/streaming.
- **AI-assisted creation from news** + **AI resolution proposals** (OpenRouter infra) — 10× the market supply at near-zero cost.

## Phase 4 — Social & creator economy (both are weak here)
- **Creators earn a fee cut** on their markets → incentivized, self-sustaining supply.
- **Copy-trading, follow, calibration/reputation scores, leaderboards.**
- **Distribution everywhere:** embeds/widgets, Discord (herald), Telegram, public API. Markets go where the audience already is.

## Phase 5 — Trust & regulatory edge
- **On-chain trustless resolution (UMA)** — more credibly neutral than Kalshi's centralized settlement.
- **Regulatory arbitrage as a feature:** play money = safe in restricted jurisdictions; on-chain = permissionless globally. We route users to the legal mode automatically.
- Self-custodial, transparent, verifiable.

## Sequencing (highest leverage first)
1. **UI polish + mobile** (own the experience — in progress).
2. **Onboarding funnel** (guest play, social login, embedded/gasless wallet).
3. **Hybrid liquidity** (CPMM+CLOB router + MM bot on every market + rebates).
4. **AI market factory** (news → markets, at scale).
5. **Creator fees + copy-trading + embeds** (supply + distribution flywheel).

The moat is the **dual-mode funnel**: we capture users at zero friction with play money, then convert the serious ones to trustless on-chain money — a bridge neither Kalshi nor Polymarket can build without becoming the other.
